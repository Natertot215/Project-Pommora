### Spaces

**Spaces are tier-1 Contexts.** This doc is retained as a stub redirect — the full spec for Spaces (and Topics, Projects, and the Contexts tier system) lives in `Contexts.md`.

Spaces are stored as `.space.json` files at `.nexus/spaces/` with composed-blocks pages (same `blocks` field shape as Topics and Projects). All three tiers share the same composed-page surface pattern.

For the singleton dashboard surface (the user's general home page that can embed anything), see `Homepage.md`.
